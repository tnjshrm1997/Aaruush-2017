Hacktrack

Ethical Hacking workshop by Sunny Vaghela


The world is involved in a cyber arms race in which professional hackers and cyber criminals are on the front foot. Ethical hacking is performed to identify potential threats on the computer or network. An ethical hacker attempts to hack their way past the system security, finding any weak points in the security that could be exploited by other hackers. Ethical hacker finds ways to improve the system security, in an effort to minimize, if not eliminate any potential hacker attacks.

By: Sunny Vaghela
