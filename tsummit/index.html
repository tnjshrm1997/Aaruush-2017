About


SRM University has always taken strides towards technological advancements and fulfilling the idea of the the notion of Innovation and completing this idea of the University, the Technical Summit (T-Summit) intends to contribute to the society by developing innovative ideas. Innovation is a long process that proposes an irrefutable outcome of development. T-Summit is the confabulation of the intellectual cream of the nation for sessions of unparalleled brain-storming. It will provide a common platform for fruitful interaction amongst the colleges of India - to come together and deliberate on issues pertaining to progress and development of the nation as a whole.
